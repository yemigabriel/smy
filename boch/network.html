Your network is the backbone of your operations - without the right infrastructure in place, you can’t operate productively or profitably. This makes network management decisions amongst the most important the IT departments will make.
New technologies and smarter ways of working can help improve business profitability. But this sort of change isn’t possible without a network capable of supporting your operational and technical ambitions. Boch’s network practice helps you optimise across the physical and virtualised environments of the data centre, campus and enterprise networks.
Complete Network Support
The complexity of networks means that processes and technologies are always evolving. Understanding and implementing the right changes is a huge drain on resources. Boch offers vendor-neutral, impartial advice to help you find the right solutions for your business.

  • In our Network Health Check we assess your hardware, software, network design, equipment lifecycles, and support coverage. This allows us to make fully informed recommendations
  • Covering physical and virtualised software-based solutions, we deliver architectural consultation and design in adherence with industry best practices
  • Our certified implementation engineers are trained to industry-leading standards, and configure and deploy Network, Wireless and Load Balancer solutions
  • We can undertake a full wireless LAN survey covering indoor and outdoor areas
  • .

Comprehensive Expertise
Network management requires that you keep on top of a vast range of technologies, most of which are constantly changing.  Boch’s team of consultants and engineers are trained in the latest technologies from the largest vendors, so you’re always ahead of the curve.

  • Our team undertakes regular expert-level training, and we hold a wide range of accreditations  that enable us to work across almost any vendor technology
  • Boch provides a full range of services, from pre-sales consultation through to the design and implementation of the network solution
  • Our services span the physical and virtualized environments of the data center, campus and enterprise networks
  • Boch’s networking engineers are certified to the highest level with our vendor partners with expert focus on technologies from Cisco and HP.

Boch is committed to helping you optimize your network to meet and every demand from the business.
At Boch, we’re focused on creating the right network solution for each of our customers. Get in touch to find out more about how Boch can help with your network management challenges.
