Company Overview

Boch Systems Company Limited is the leading information availability company in Africa. We are industry leaders in providing quality standards based infrastructure and technology solutions to a wide range of sectors.

Boch Systems was incorporated in 2006 by a team of seasoned executives with vast experience and pedigree on service delivery in Africa, Headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria. It became a limited company since 2006 and since been backed by a management team with extensive Knowledge and experience in information technology services and solutions, including infrastructure deployment, Enterprise solutions software and high levels services delivery.

We work directly with manufacturers or wholesale partners to ensure a fast turn around and delivery-from-stock fulfilment on most items and also we have in-house trained sales and IT staffs, with in-depth knowledge of the products that we supply Over 50% of our staff strength has a core role in services delivery, support and implementation.
