Physical security threats are no longer the only – or even the primary – threats to your company. The risks businesses face from data leakages or more malicious cybercrime mean the importance of a comprehensive IT security solution is unprecedented. 
Security solutions are complex but necessary technologies. To ensure they operate effectively however, you need specialist knowledge, and for many organisations keeping on top of this skill set is too costly and resource-intensive. Boch’s security practice offers you complete support, from the design stage into deployment, and then throughout the lifestyle of your solution.
Defending your Organisation
It’s impossible to predict every possible threat that might target your organisation. Boch understands this challenge, having worked with hundreds of customers, vendors and partners. Because every infrastructure is different, our team of consultants and engineers are vendor-agnostic, building solutions based on the outcomes required and not specific products.

  • Boch’s team is trained in an extensive vendor portfolio, including: Fortinet, HP, Symantec, EMC and NNTWS
  • To ensure we deliver the most appropriate solution, we undertake extensive compliance, governance and security integrity analysis with our customers
  • Boch  provides ISO 27001 Compliance Assessment, business impact analysis, and threat and risk assessments – offering maximum security and compliance visibility for your operations
  • Our solutions are backed up by our dedicated technical support team and industry-leading solutions architects.

Creating a Secure Environment
When deploying your security policies, you want certainty that you’re safeguarding your business in the right way. Boch’s Solutions Architects design tailored security solutions which meet the unique needs of each business.

  • Whatever the shape of your infrastructure, we can identify the right security solution, encompassing: access, identity & vulnerability control firewalls (UTM & NGFW); IDS/IPS; Network Access Control (NAC); Virtualisation; and Cloud Security
  • Kelway specialises in a host of security technologies, including: endpoint protection; email & web security; mobile device management (for BYOD environments); and full disk encryption
  • To maximise visibility of your challenges and threats, we offer security management & remote support (On-Premise & Cloud Based), log retention and security incident and event monitoring (SIEM). 

Boch is committed to finding the right security solution to keep your business protected.
